Photography

Amongst other things, I am a photographer. I work primarily on black and white film using a 4x5 Shen Hao wooden field camera. In general, I like to photograph people, or at least items related to people.

Since I work primarily on film, not all of my projects are suitable for digitization and viewing on a screen. When I do digitize my work, it is normally by scanning the negatives and then doing some minor post processing. Occasionally, I photograph or scan my prints. Either way, the work shown here is not an exhaustive representation of my work. If you want to see that, you'll need to schedule a meeting to look at my analog portfolio.
